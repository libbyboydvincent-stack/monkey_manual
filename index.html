Why Monkey's Brain Works Like This

Everything neurotypical brains do on autopilot — regulate emotions, start things, stop things — ADHD brains do manually. That is exhausting in a way that is invisible from the outside.

Fluctuating oestrogen affects dopamine regulation, amplifying emotional sensitivity and brain fog, making manual regulation even harder.

Often neither of you will understand why today is a bad day, and that's okay. This is not your fault or responsibility. But showing up makes a real difference.
