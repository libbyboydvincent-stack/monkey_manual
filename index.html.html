Critical Safety Notice — Read Before Proceeding

DO NOT attempt to tease Monkey out of a bad mood. Even if it feels kind, playful teasing lands as evidence she is not being taken seriously — and that is the exact fear that is already active in her brain right now. Her nervous system hears it as confirmation, not contradiction.

Put the stick down. We are trying something different. Start below. 👇

💜

Why Monkey's brain works like this

Monkey has ADHD and is in perimenopause. These interact: fluctuating oestrogen affects dopamine regulation, which amplifies emotional sensitivity, brain fog, and overwhelm — sometimes unpredictably. Some bad days have no obvious cause. That is normal and not your fault. Your job is not to fix it or understand it. Your job is to be a safe harbour while it passes.
